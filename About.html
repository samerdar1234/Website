Sam I am

Greetings! I am Sam Fitch, a senior at the University of Michigan.

An intuitively curious problem solver with a keen ability to bring technical and non-technical personalities together, I am pursuing a major in Computer Science.

Computers are everywhere and technology is our new frontier. I was drawn to Computer Science because I love puzzles and creative problem solving. I also like the fact that it is the great equilizer; code is code- computers are blind to everyone and everything but code, and nothing can hold you back from creating something exceptional and useful.

An ardent believer in the ethical use of technology as a platform for positive change, personal growth, and professional advancement, I am additionally pursuing a minor in Digital Communications.

During my free time you can find me knitting, baking, or spending time with family, friends, and my cat.
